OpenFin Commercial Codec Support

How to enable commercial codecs within your OpenFin application.

This example includes 3 news websites that stream content using commercial codecs that are not enabled in OpenFin by default. This how-to shows you were you can put your settings in your manifest to enable commercial codecs. Without this you will not be able play any of the videos. Once you have provided this information then the video will play.
For this to work you need the latest version of runtime v38+ and RVM 15 or higher.
